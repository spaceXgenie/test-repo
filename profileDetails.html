Okoro Gladys Ajiri

creative director,

Gladys is a Lagos based interior designer with over Five years of residential and commercial experience. She established her own Interior design and construction company where she has worked in the past five years as the lead project Manager on many residential projects for various individuals and companies. She holds a Certification from the CEE school of Interior Design, Dubai. She is also passionate about raising and inspiring people as a Hope coach through her life story and experience which have helped build her to what she has become. Over the past years, Gladys has brought her eclectic style to various homes, offices and a variety of projects, including restaurants and bars, modern homes in Lekki, hotel chains and others. She has worked with brands such as Mainstreet Bank, Halogen Securities, Oracle Nigeria, Ogun State Government, IBM and many more to create an exquisite finish. She has extensive experience working on residential and hospitality projects. She credits her background in design to her success at DW-Muree, an architectural firm and Afrykey Konsult. Her experiences gave her an appreciation for luxurious details, quality craftsmanship and a splash of the unexpected. She has obtained awards and special mentions with her team of architects and engineers. Her designs have been featured in a couple of newspapers including This Day and Business day Nigeria, where she was profiled as a “Rising Star” in the design industry. Gladys describes her vision as ecstatic, stylish and classic. A world-traveler, she draws inspiration from a variety of cultural, professional, and artistic contexts, which she blends into her unique design style.


She brings together the chicness of the feminine gender and the drive of the lagosian to curate one of a kind piece’s that tell stories and create spaces of lasting curiosity. As an experienced interior designer she believes she can help paint the possibilities of an empty room. Whether it be modern, transitional or traditional, she is a resource to transform a space into a home.
